The Aim Of The Game

The aim of the game is to collect as many coins and defeat as many enemies as possible to accumulate your score, whilst also being against the clock. The faster you complete the level, the more additional points you will get upon completion too. However, should these treacherous enemies get to close and hit you, you shall face the consequences so be sure to try and avoid these at all costs. Use the blocks within the level to traverse your way through and avoid as much imminent danger as possible to make your life easier. If you are skilled enough to reach the end of the level, be sure to reach as high on the pole as possible to get every last point available to you at this point.

The Background

The background within my project is one that I created myself but taking various assets online and using editing software to create the background. This then allowed me to create a long enough asset for a scrolling background.
