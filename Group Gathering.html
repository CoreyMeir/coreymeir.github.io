Group Gathering

Group gathering is a turn-based, party, trivia game in which you must take on the challenge of going up against your friends and family to take all of the bragging rights. Whilst traversing across the map from a dice roll, you need to try and maximise your points to outscore your opponents whilst also avoiding certain rolls that will take you backwards. Created in Unity using C#.

The Players

You can play with up to 3 other people in the initial release of the game. With the choice of 5 different skins.

Number of Players

Select how many players you would like to be involved in your intense game of Group Gathering

Skin Selection

Choose from the variety of skins to customise your character and create your legacy of being the ultimate winner.

The Dice

This is the centre piece to the journey you will take to success, with a randomly generated number which will decide your fate on which space you land on. The only thing left to do, is for you to answer the question correctly.

The Map

The map is a series of blocks on a plane in which it is divided into four sections. Each section defines a different trivia area and will determine the type of question you receive when landing on a trivia block.

Score and Winning

The score you accumulate is done by answering the questions as quickly as possible and also landing on bonus blocks. The outcome is simple, the player with the most score wins... so make sure this is you.
