Hunt of Salvation

The name of the game is Hunt of Salvation, and the objective is to kill all enemies around you, whilst completing objectives and gaining achievements to keep growing stronger. The stronger you become, the more realms that will be unlocked and will allow you to tackle harder enemies and dominate the dis-utopian world of Kubreaghar.
